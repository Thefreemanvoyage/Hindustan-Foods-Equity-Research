# Hindustan Foods Ltd — Equity Research & Valuation

A complete, self built Equity research project on **Hindustan Foods Limited
(NSE: HNDFDS)** — India's largest listed FMCG contract manufacturer. The project covers
the full analyst workflow: building an integrated financial model from historicals through
a five-year forecast, valuing the company through multiple methods, and presenting the
findings as a research report with an investment recommendation.

captured), steady EBITDA-margin expansion (~5.7% → ~8.7%, FY22–FY26), and a valuation
discount to branded-FMCG peers that leaves room for re-rating.

## The financial model — sheet by sheet

**Forecast drivers**
- `ASSUMPTIONS` — central driver sheet (growth, margins, tax, capex, working-capital
  assumptions) feeding the rest of the model.

**Three-statement model**
- `INCOME STATEMENT` — revenue through to PAT, forecast over the projection horizon.
- `BALANCE SHEET` — assets, liabilities, and equity, kept in balance with the other statements.
- `CFS` — Cash Flow Statement (operating, investing, financing), linking the IS and BS.

**Supporting schedules**
- `FA SCHEDULE` — fixed-asset and depreciation schedule (capex roll-forward, D&A).
- `DEBT SCHEDULE` — opening/closing debt, interest, and split of short- vs long-term debt.

**Cost of capital**
- `WACC` — weighted average cost of capital build-up.
- `REGRESSION BETA` — beta estimated by regressing the stock against the market.
- `BOTTOM UP BETA` — beta built from peer betas, unlevered and relevered to HFL's capital structure.

**Valuation**
- `DCF VALUATION` — discounted cash flow: FCFF projected FY26F–FY30F, terminal value, and
  discounting at WACC (~8.5%) to an equity value per share (~₹760).
- `COMPANY COMPARABLE ANALYSIS` — relative valuation vs CCL Products, Tasty Bite, Avanti
  Feeds, Mrs. Bectors, and Zydus Wellness, using EV/Sales, EV/EBITDA, EV/EBIT, and P/E
  (with median, quartile, and average multiples).
- `(INPUT SHEET)` — sensitivity / scenario analysis: flexes terminal growth (3.5%–4.5%)
  and exit multiple (21×–22×) to produce a valuation range for each method.
- `FOOTBALL FIELD ANALYSIS` — synthesises every method (DCF, comps, EV/Sales, P/E,
  52-week range, unaffected price) into a single valuation range to frame the recommendation.

## What I did

- Built a fully **integrated three statement model** (IS, BS, CFS) driven off a single
  assumptions sheet, with linked fixed-asset and debt schedules.
- Estimated **cost of capital** independently, including beta by **two methods**
  (regression and bottom-up) for cross-validation.
- Performed an intrinsic **DCF valuation** (FCFF, terminal value, WACC discounting).
- Built a **relative valuation** against six listed peers across four multiples.
- Ran **sensitivity analysis** on the key value drivers and consolidated all approaches in
  a **football-field** to set a defensible target.
- Authored a **13-slide research report** translating the analysis into an investment
  thesis, risk assessment, and BUY recommendation.
